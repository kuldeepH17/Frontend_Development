Indian cricket team members

Virat Kohli

Qualification: Virat Kohli completed his schooling from Vishal Bharti Public School in Delhi.

Experience: As of 2021, Virat Kohli was the captain of the Indian cricket team and one of the world's leading batsmen. He has achieved numerous records and has been a key player for the Indian team.

Rohit Sharma

Qualification: Rohit Sharma completed his schooling from Swami Vivekanand International School in Mumbai.

Experience: Rohit Sharma is a vice-captain of the Indian cricket team and is known for his explosive batting. He has several centuries in international cricket.

Ajinkya Rahane

Qualification: Ajinkya Rahane completed his schooling from SV Joshi High School in Mumbai.

Experience: Rahane is a seasoned batsman who has been a part of the Indian team for many years. He is known for his solid technique and has captained the team on various occasions.

Ravichandran Ashwin

Qualification: Ravichandran Ashwin completed his engineering degree in Information Technology from SSN College of Engineering in Chennai.

Experience: Ashwin is a premier spinner in the Indian team and has achieved numerous milestones in Test cricket. He is known for his bowling variations.

Jasprit Bumrah

Qualification: Jasprit Bumrah has not pursued higher education after completing his schooling.

Experience: Bumrah is one of India's leading fast bowlers known for his exceptional pace and yorkers. He made a rapid rise in international cricket.

Cheteshwar Pujara

Qualification: Cheteshwar Pujara completed his BBA degree from Saurashtra University.

Experience: Pujara is a specialist Test batsman who has played crucial innings for India in the longer format of the game. He is known for his resilience and technique.
